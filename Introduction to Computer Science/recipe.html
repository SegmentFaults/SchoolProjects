Chocolate Chip Cookies

This a very tasty chocolate chip cookie recipe. It tastes really good.

This should make enough cookies for about 5 people to have a square cookie meal.Betty Crocker states that this recipe should take about an hour to make.

Ingredients

  • Three Quarters cups of granulated sugar
  • Three quarters cups of packed brown sugar
  • 1 cup butter or margerine, softened
  • 1 teaspoon of vanilla
  • 1 egg
  • 9 quarter cups of flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1 half teaspoon of salt
  • 2 cups of semisweet chocolate chips

Directions

  1. Heat oven to 375
  2. Mix sugars, butter, vanilla and egg in large bowl. Stir in flour, baking soda and salt (dough will be stiff). Stir in nuts and chocolate chips.
  3. Drop dough by rounded tablespoonfuls about 2 inches apart onto ungreased cookie sheet.
  4. Bake 8 to 10 minutes or until light brown. Cool slightly; remove from cookie sheet. Cool on wire rack.
